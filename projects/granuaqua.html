Granu Aqua Project

The Granu Aqua Project is developed to manage and monitor water supply operations efficiently for residential, commercial, and industrial use. The system helps in maintaining records related to water distribution, quality monitoring, billing, and customer management in an organized manner. It supports accurate tracking of water usage, timely service delivery, and proper maintenance scheduling. By reducing manual work and improving data accuracy, the project promotes efficient water resource management and ensures transparency in operations. Overall, the Granu Aqua Project provides a reliable and sustainable solution for modern water management systems.
